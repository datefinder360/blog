The Ideal Length of a First Date: 90 Minutes

Dating can be a nerve-wracking experience, and one of the biggest questions is how long should a first date last? According to a dating etiquette expert, the ideal first date length is 90 minutes in order to determine if there is a spark.

Ryan Seacrest, the famous radio host, recently shared the amount of time your first date should last and that is 90 minutes. This length of time is long enough to gauge if there is a spark between the two of you, but also short enough for your date to want to see you again, or vice versa.

Benefits of a 90-Minute Date

• Gives you enough time to get to know each other

• Allows for a more relaxed atmosphere

• Helps to reduce the pressure of a first date

• Gives you a chance to connect and build a rapport

• Allows for a natural flow of conversation

• Gives you an opportunity to find out if there is a spark

Tips for Making the Most of a 90-Minute Date

• Don’t be afraid to ask questions

• Show genuine interest in your date

• Keep the conversation light and fun

• Avoid talking about exes or past relationships

• Don’t be afraid to share stories and anecdotes

• Don’t be afraid to be yourself

• Don’t be afraid to take the conversation to the next level

• Don’t be afraid to take a break if the conversation starts to lag

A 90-minute date is the ideal length of time for a first date. It is long enough to get to know each other and determine if there is a spark, but also short enough for your date to want to see you again. Follow the tips above to make the most of your 90-minute date and have a great time!
