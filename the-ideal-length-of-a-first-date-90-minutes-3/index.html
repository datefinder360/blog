The Ideal Length of a First Date: 90 Minutes

When it comes to first dates, it can be difficult to determine how long it should last. Dating etiquette expert Ryan Seacrest recently shared the ideal length of a first date in order to determine if there is a spark. According to Seacrest, the ideal length of a first date is 90 minutes.

Why 90 Minutes?

The 90-minute mark is the perfect length for a first date because it is long enough to gauge if there is a spark between two people, but also short enough that both parties will want to see each other again. This length of time allows for enough conversation to get to know one another, but not too much time to become bored or uncomfortable.

What to Do During the Date

When it comes to what to do during the date, it is important to keep it light and fun. It is a good idea to plan activities that allow for conversation and connection, such as a coffee date, a walk in the park, or a game night. It is also important to remember to be respectful and courteous throughout the date.

Tips for Making the Most of the Date

In order to make the most of the 90-minute date, it is important to be mindful of the time. Set a timer if necessary to ensure that the date does not go over the allotted time. Additionally, it is important to stay focused on the conversation and to be open and honest with one another. Finally, it is important to remember to have fun and enjoy the date.

When it comes to first dates, the ideal length is 90 minutes. This length of time allows for enough conversation to get to know one another, but not too much time to become bored or uncomfortable. It is important to plan activities that allow for conversation and connection, and to be mindful of the time. With these tips, you can make the most of your 90-minute date and determine if there is a spark between you and your date.
