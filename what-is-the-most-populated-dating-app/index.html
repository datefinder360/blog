What is the most populated dating app?


Dating apps have become a popular way to meet potential partners, with over 300 million people using them worldwide. In 2021, the dating app market made $5.61 billion in revenue, with almost $3 billion of that coming from Match Group. But which dating app is the most popular?

Dating App Key Statistics

According to Statista, in 2021, Tinder was the most downloaded dating app, with over 500 million downloads. Bumble came in second, with over 400 million downloads. In terms of revenue, Match Group was the clear leader, making almost $3 billion. Other popular dating apps include Hinge, OkCupid, and Plenty of Fish.

In terms of user demographics, most dating app users are aged 18-34, with a slight majority of users being female. Over 20 million people pay for premium features on dating apps, such as additional filters and unlimited swipes.

Safety is an important factor when it comes to dating apps. Most apps have safety features such as the ability to block and report users, as well as in-app moderation. Additionally, many apps are now offering video chat features, allowing users to get to know each other better before meeting in person.
