What should you not say on a dating site?

Online dating is a great way to meet potential partners, but it can also be a minefield of faux pas and bad decisions. When it comes to creating a profile, it's important to be aware of what you should and shouldn't say. Here are five things you should never put on a dating profile.

What You're *Not* Looking For

When it comes to creating a dating profile, it's easy to get carried away and start listing all the things you don't want in a partner. However, this can be a major turn-off for potential matches. Instead of listing the traits you don't want, focus on the qualities you do want in a partner.

Anything Too Generic

It's important to make your profile stand out from the crowd. Writing generic statements such as "I'm looking for someone who loves to laugh" or "I'm looking for someone who enjoys the outdoors" won't do much to make you stand out. Instead, focus on writing something unique and interesting about yourself.

Sarcasm About Being on 'The Apps'

It's easy to get jaded when it comes to online dating, but it's important to remember that everyone is in the same boat. Making sarcastic comments about being on the apps won't do much to make you stand out from the crowd.

Lies (or Even Exaggerations)

It's important to be honest when creating a dating profile. Lying or exaggerating about your age, job, or interests won't do you any favors in the long run. Not only will it make it harder to find a compatible match, but it could also lead to an awkward first date.

*No Information*

It's important to include enough information in your profile to give potential matches an idea of who you are. Leaving your profile blank or with minimal information won't do much to attract potential matches.

Creating a dating profile can be a daunting task, but it's important to be aware of what you should and shouldn't say. Avoiding the five things listed above will help ensure that your profile stands out from the crowd and that you find a compatible match. By following these tips, you'll be sure to make a great first impression on potential matches.
