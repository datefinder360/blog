How to Know When a Man is Done with You

When it comes to relationships, it can be difficult to know when a man is done with you. It can be even harder to accept that a relationship is over, falling apart and possibly beyond repair. Knowing the signs of a man being done with you can help you to move on and start to heal.

Signs He's Done With You
There are several signs that a man is done with you. These include:

He Becomes Evasive: If your man is becoming evasive and avoiding anything to do with you, this is a sign that he is done with the relationship. He may be avoiding conversations about the relationship, or he may be avoiding spending time with you.

He Pushes You Away When You Want to Get Close: If your man is pushing you away when you want to get close, this is a sign that he is done with the relationship. He may be avoiding physical contact, or he may be avoiding spending time with you.

He Doesn't Show Up When He Promised: If your man is no longer showing up when he promised, this is a sign that he is done with the relationship. He may be canceling plans at the last minute, or he may be avoiding spending time with you.

He Can't Be Bothered to Be 'Nice': If your man is no longer making an effort to be 'nice' to you, this is a sign that he is done with the relationship. He may be making rude comments or jokes, or he may be avoiding spending time with you.

He Is Mean to You: If your man is being mean to you, this is a sign that he is done with the relationship. He may be making rude comments or jokes, or he may be avoiding spending time with you.

If you are noticing any of these signs in your relationship, it is likely that your man is done with you. It can be difficult to accept that a relationship is over, but knowing the signs can help you to move on and start to heal.
