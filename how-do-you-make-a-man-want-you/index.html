How do you make a man want you?

Here are some tips and tricks from relationship and matchmaking experts that can help you get a man to focus his attention on you:

• Look him in the eye. When you’re talking to him, make sure you look him in the eye. This will show him that you’re confident and interested in what he has to say.

• Avoid the obvious. Don’t be too obvious when you’re trying to get his attention. If you’re too obvious, he may think you’re desperate and not take you seriously.

• Go out alone or with one other friend. Going out with a group of friends can be intimidating for a man. If you want to get his attention, try going out alone or with one other friend.
