Surviving a Sexless Marriage: How to Cope


A sexless marriage can be a difficult situation to navigate, and it can be hard to know how to survive it. Fortunately, there are steps you can take to cope with a sexless marriage and help your relationship to thrive.

Pick Your Moment to Talk
The first step to surviving a sexless marriage is to pick the right moment to talk. It is important to find a time when both of you are relaxed and can have an honest and open conversation. It is also important to be respectful and understanding of each other’s feelings.

Pick Your Moment to Listen
In addition to picking the right moment to talk, it is also important to pick the right moment to listen. It is important to be patient and to listen to your partner’s feelings and concerns. It is also important to be open to hearing their point of view and to be willing to compromise.

Be Honest With Yourself and Each Other
It is important to be honest with yourself and each other about the situation. It is important to be honest about your feelings and your needs. It is also important to be honest about what you are willing to do to make the situation better.

Decide Whether Sex is a Deal-Breaker
It is important to decide whether sex is a deal-breaker for either of you. If it is, then it is important to be honest about it and to find a way to move forward. If it is not, then it is important to be willing to work together to find a solution.

Be Patient
It is important to be patient with each other. It is important to remember that it may take time to work through the issues and to find a solution. It is also important to be patient with yourself and to remember that it is okay to take your time.

Seek Help Together
It is important to seek help together. There are many resources available to help couples who are struggling with a sexless marriage. It is important to be willing to explore these resources and to be open to trying new things.

Kindness is Sexy
It is important to remember that kindness is sexy. It is important to be kind to each other and to show each other love and affection. It is also important to be patient and understanding of each other’s needs.

Ban Sex
Finally, it is important to ban sex. It is important to remember that sex is not the only way to show love and affection. It is important to focus on other ways to show love and to be intimate with each other.

Surviving a sexless marriage can be a difficult situation to navigate, but it is possible. It is important to pick the right moment to talk and to listen, to be honest with yourself and each other, to decide whether sex is a deal-breaker, to be patient, to seek help together, to be kind, and to ban sex. With the right approach, it is possible to cope with a sexless marriage and to help your relationship to thrive.
