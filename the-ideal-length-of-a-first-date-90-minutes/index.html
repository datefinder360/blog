The Ideal Length of a First Date: 90 Minutes


Going on a first date can be a nerve-wracking experience. You want to make a good impression, but you also want to make sure that you and your date have enough time to get to know each other. A dating etiquette expert recently shared the ideal length of a first date in order to determine if there is a spark.

Ryan Seacrest shared that the ideal length of a first date should be 90 minutes. This is long enough to get to know each other and determine if there is a spark, but also short enough to leave your date wanting more.

Tips for Making the Most of Your First Date

• Plan Ahead: Planning ahead can help ensure that your date goes smoothly. Make sure you have a plan for the date and that you know where you are going.

• Be Open-Minded: Don’t be afraid to try something new or go somewhere you’ve never been before. This can help keep the conversation flowing and make the date more interesting.

• Ask Questions: Asking questions is a great way to get to know your date better. Ask about their interests, hobbies, and goals.

• Be Positive: Try to stay positive and upbeat throughout the date. This will help create a more enjoyable atmosphere and make it easier to connect with your date.

• Have Fun: Don’t forget to have fun! Enjoy the moment and make sure to laugh and smile.

• Follow Up: After the date, make sure to follow up with your date. This can be a simple text or call to thank them for the date and let them know you had a good time.

A first date can be a nerve-wracking experience, but with the right preparation and attitude, it can be a great opportunity to get to know someone. Ryan Seacrest shared that the ideal length of a first date should be 90 minutes. This is long enough to gauge if there is a spark, but also short enough for your date to want to see you again. By following the tips above, you can make the most of your first date and hopefully create a connection that will last.
