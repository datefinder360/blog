What Not to Wear on a First Date: A Guide to Making a Good Impression


When you’re preparing for a first date, it’s important to make sure you look your best. You don’t want to give off the impression that you’re frumpy or sloppy. It’s important to choose clothing that is appropriate for the occasion and shows that you put effort into your appearance. Here are some tips on what not to wear on a first date.

Clothing with Holes, Stains, or Pilling

Avoid any clothing materials with holes, stains, or pilling. Your first impression should be neat, clean and borderline professional. That's a really difficult look to pull off in tattered clothes, even if they are old favorites! Even if you don’t want to dress up too much, you should still make sure your clothes are free of any damage or wear.

Clothing that is Too Revealing

It’s important to dress appropriately for the occasion. If you’re going on a first date, you don’t want to wear something that is too revealing. Save the skimpy outfits for a night out with your friends. You should also avoid wearing clothing that is too tight or too short.

Clothing that is Too Casual

On the other hand, you don’t want to wear something that is too casual. A first date is not the time to wear your favorite pair of sweatpants or a t-shirt. You should opt for something that is a bit more dressy, such as a nice dress or a pair of slacks and a blouse.

Clothing that is Too Formal

You also don’t want to wear something that is too formal. If you’re going to a nice restaurant, you don’t need to wear a tuxedo or a ball gown. A nice dress or a pair of slacks and a blouse is appropriate for most first dates.

Clothing that is Too Trendy

Finally, you don’t want to wear something that is too trendy. You don’t want to look like you’re trying too hard. Stick to classic pieces that will never go out of style.

When you’re getting ready for a first date, it’s important to make sure you’re wearing the right clothing. Avoid any clothing materials with holes, stains, or pilling. You should also avoid clothing that is too revealing, too casual, too formal, or too trendy. Your first impression should be neat, clean and borderline professional. By following these tips, you can make sure you look your best and make a good impression on your first date.
