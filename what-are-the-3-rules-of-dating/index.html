What are the 3 rules of dating?


Dating can be a tricky business, but there are some basic rules that can help you navigate the waters and make sure you end up with a successful relationship. Here are the three golden rules of dating that you should always keep in mind.

A SEO-title: 3 Dating Rules to Live By

Dating is a process of getting to know someone and deciding if you want to enter into a relationship with them. It can be an exciting and fun experience, but it can also be a bit overwhelming. To make sure you have the best possible experience, it’s important to follow a few basic rules.

Rule #1: If He’s Interested in You, You’ll Know

One of the most important rules of dating is to trust your instincts. If a guy is interested in you, he will make it known. He will call you, text you, and make plans to see you. If you find yourself agonizing over what to text him or picking apart every bit of communication to try and figure out why he’s not calling, it’s a sign that he’s not interested.

Rule #2: When It’s Right, It’s Easy

Dating should be fun and enjoyable. If you’re constantly worrying about how to act or what to say, it’s a sign that the relationship isn’t right for you. When you’re with the right person, it will feel easy and natural. You won’t have to try too hard to make it work.

Rule #3: Don’t Do Things You’re Not Comfortable With

It’s important to respect your own boundaries and not do anything that makes you uncomfortable. If a guy is pressuring you to do something you don’t want to do, it’s a sign that he’s not the right person for you.

Dating can be a fun and exciting experience, but it’s important to follow a few basic rules. Trust your instincts, make sure it feels right, and don’t do anything that makes you uncomfortable. By following these three simple rules, you can ensure that you have a successful and enjoyable dating experience.
