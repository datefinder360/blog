Is Your Marriage Sexless?



Sex is an important part of any marriage. It can bring couples closer together and strengthen their bond. Unfortunately, many marriages end up sexless. But what does it mean to be sexless and how can couples avoid it?

What is a Sexless Marriage?

A sexless marriage is defined as a marriage where the couple has not had sex with each other in the past six months to one year. According to one study, approximately 15 percent of married couples are sexless. This means that a large number of couples are struggling with a lack of physical intimacy in their relationship.

What Causes a Sexless Marriage?

There are many reasons why a marriage may become sexless. Some of the most common causes include stress, depression, physical health issues, lack of communication, and a lack of trust. Other factors such as busy schedules, children, and financial problems can also contribute to a lack of physical intimacy in a marriage.

How to Avoid a Sexless Marriage

The best way to avoid a sexless marriage is to make sure that you and your partner are communicating openly and honestly about your physical needs. It is also important to make time for each other and to prioritize your relationship. Additionally, couples should make sure that they are taking care of their physical and mental health, as this can have a big impact on their sex life.

Sex is an important part of any marriage and it is important to make sure that you and your partner are communicating openly and honestly about your physical needs. By taking the time to prioritize your relationship and taking care of your physical and mental health, you can help to ensure that your marriage remains healthy and happy.
