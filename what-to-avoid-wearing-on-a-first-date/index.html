What to Avoid Wearing on a First Date

First dates can be nerve-wracking, and the last thing you want to worry about is what to wear. To make sure you make the best impression, here are some tips on what not to wear on a first date.

No Holes, Stains, or Pilling

When it comes to making a good first impression, you don't want to give off the impression that you're frumpy or sloppy. Avoid any clothing materials with holes, stains, or pilling. Your first impression should be neat, clean and borderline professional. That's a really difficult look to pull off in tattered clothes, even if they are old favorites!

No Overly Revealing Clothes

It's important to dress to impress, but you don't want to be too revealing. You want to look attractive, but you don't want to give off the impression that you're trying too hard. Stick to clothes that are form-fitting but not overly tight.

No Uncomfortable Shoes

Your shoes are an important part of your outfit, and you don't want to be uncomfortable on your date. Avoid wearing shoes that are too tight or too loose. Stick to shoes that are comfortable and stylish.

No Overly Casual Attire

While you don't want to be too formal, you also don't want to be too casual. Avoid wearing overly casual attire such as sweatpants, t-shirts, and flip-flops. Stick to clothes that are more dressy, such as a nice pair of jeans and a blouse.

No Too Much Fragrance

You want to smell nice, but you don't want to wear too much fragrance. Stick to light scents that are not too overpowering. You don't want to give off the impression that you're trying too hard.

No Too Much Makeup

Makeup can be a great way to enhance your features, but you don't want to wear too much. Stick to light makeup that is natural-looking. You don't want to look like you're trying too hard.

When it comes to what not to wear on a first date, it's important to keep it neat, clean, and professional. Avoid any clothing materials with holes, stains, or pilling. Stick to clothes that are form-fitting but not overly tight. Wear comfortable shoes and avoid overly casual attire. Make sure you don't wear too much fragrance or makeup. Following these tips will help you make the best impression on your first date.
