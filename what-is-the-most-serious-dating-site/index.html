FriendFinder is a great option for those looking for a mix of casual and serious relationships. The site offers a variety of features, including chat rooms, message boards, and video chat. FriendFinder also has a detailed search feature that allows users to find matches based on specific criteria.

OkCupid: Best for Progressive Dating

OkCupid is a great choice for those looking for progressive dating. The site offers a variety of features, including detailed profiles, match suggestions, and a unique "Questions" feature that allows users to answer questions about themselves and their potential matches.
