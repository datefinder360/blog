What is Roaching?

Roaching is a term used to describe a behavior in which a partner hides the fact that they are still sleeping around with other people. This behavior is usually seen early on in a relationship, when one partner is trying to hide their promiscuity. It is important to note that roaching is not necessarily a sign of cheating, but rather a sign of a lack of commitment.

The Signs of Roaching

There are several signs that can indicate roaching in a relationship. These include:

• Being secretive about their whereabouts or who they are with

• Not introducing you to their friends or family

• Not wanting to commit to plans or make long-term commitments

• Making excuses for why they can’t be with you

• Refusing to answer questions about their past relationships

• Being evasive when asked about their feelings for you

The Dangers of Roaching

Roaching can be dangerous for a relationship because it can lead to feelings of mistrust and insecurity. If one partner is not being honest about their behavior, it can lead to a lack of trust and a feeling of being taken advantage of. This can lead to a breakdown in communication and can ultimately lead to the end of the relationship.

How to Deal with Roaching
