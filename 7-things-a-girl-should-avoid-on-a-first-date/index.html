7 Things a Girl Should Avoid on a First Date

First dates can be a nerve-wracking experience for anyone, especially for girls. It is important to make a good impression on your date, so here are seven things a girl should avoid on a first date.

Be Rude to Staff

When you are out on a date, it is important to remember to be polite to the staff. Being rude to waiters, bartenders, or any other staff members will not make a good impression on your date. It is important to be respectful and kind to everyone you meet.

Constantly Check Your Phone

It is understandable to check your phone once or twice during a date, but it is important to not be glued to your phone. Constantly checking your phone will make it seem like you are not interested in your date and it can be a major turn off.

Talk About Your Ex

Talking about your ex on a first date is a major no-no. This will make it seem like you are still hung up on your ex and it will not make a good impression on your date.

Swear Too Much

It is important to watch your language on a first date. Swearing too much can make you seem unrefined and it can make your date uncomfortable.

Refuse to Pay Your Way

It is important to offer to pay your way on a first date. Refusing to pay your way can make you seem ungrateful and it can make your date feel like you are taking advantage of them.

Turn Up Late

It is important to be on time for a first date. Turning up late will make it seem like you are not taking the date seriously and it can make your date feel disrespected.

Constantly Check Your Appearance

It is important to make sure you look presentable on a first date, but it is important to not be constantly checking your appearance. Constantly checking your appearance can make it seem like you are not confident in yourself and it can make your date feel uncomfortable.

Come On Too Strong

It is important to be yourself on a first date, but it is important to not come on too strong. Coming on too strong can make your date feel uncomfortable and it can make it seem like you are not interested in getting to know them.

First dates can be a nerve-wracking experience for anyone, especially for girls. It is important to make a good impression on your date, so it is important to avoid these seven things on a first date. Be polite to staff, don't be glued to your phone, don't talk about your ex, watch your language, offer to pay your way, be on time, don't constantly check your appearance, and don't come on too strong. Following these tips will help you make a good impression on your date.
