Are straight couples more likely to meet online?


Are straight couples more likely to meet online? This is a question that has been on the minds of many people in the dating world. With the rise of online dating, it is no surprise that this is a question that has been asked. According to a study conducted by Michael J. Rosenfeld, a professor of sociology in the School of Humanities and Sciences, the answer is yes.

What the Study Found

Rosenfeld conducted a nationally representative 2017 survey of American adults and found that about 39 percent of heterosexual couples reported meeting their partner online, compared to 22 percent in 2009. This data shows that the number of straight couples meeting online has more than doubled in the past decade.

Reasons for the Increase

There are several reasons why the number of straight couples meeting online has increased. One of the most obvious reasons is the rise of online dating apps and websites. These apps and websites make it easier for people to find potential partners, and they provide a platform for people to connect with each other. Additionally, the stigma around online dating has decreased in recent years, making it more socially acceptable for people to meet online.

Benefits of Meeting Online

There are many benefits to meeting online for straight couples. One of the main benefits is that it allows people to connect with potential partners who they may not have had the opportunity to meet in person. Additionally, online dating gives people the chance to get to know someone before committing to a relationship. This can help to ensure that the couple is compatible and that the relationship has a better chance of lasting.
