How many dates before you get a serious relationship?

It's no secret that dating can be a complicated process. Whether you're looking for a casual fling or a serious relationship, it can be difficult to know how many dates you should go on before taking things to the next level. Fortunately, a 2015 dating survey conducted by Time out of 11,000 people worldwide has shed some light on the subject.

According to the survey, most couples go on 5 to 6 dates before discussing a relationship, and some take even longer. On average, people need 5-6 dates to make it official. This means that if you're looking for a serious relationship, it's important to take your time and get to know someone before making any commitments.

How to Make the Most of Your Dates

When it comes to dating, it's important to make the most of each date. To do this, you should focus on getting to know the other person and building a connection. This can be done by engaging in meaningful conversations, asking questions, and sharing stories. Additionally, it's important to be honest and open about your feelings and intentions.

It's also important to remember that each date should be enjoyable and fun. This means that you should avoid talking about heavy topics such as politics and religion, and instead focus on activities that both of you can enjoy. This could include going to the movies, taking a walk in the park, or trying a new restaurant.

When to Discuss a Relationship

Once you've gone on a few dates and feel like you've gotten to know the other person, it's time to discuss the possibility of a relationship. This can be a difficult conversation to have, but it's important to be honest and open about your feelings.

If the other person is not interested in a relationship, it's important to respect their decision and move on. However, if they are interested, it's important to discuss what kind of relationship you both want and set boundaries. This will help ensure that both of you are on the same page and that the relationship is healthy and fulfilling.
