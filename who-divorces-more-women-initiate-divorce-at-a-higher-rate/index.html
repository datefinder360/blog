2. Financial Security: Women are more likely to be financially dependent on their husbands, making them more vulnerable in the event of a divorce. This can lead to a feeling of insecurity and a desire to seek financial independence.

3. Power Dynamics: In many marriages, men are seen as the head of the household, which can lead to a feeling of powerlessness in women. This can lead to a desire to take control of their lives and seek a divorce.
