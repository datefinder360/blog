What is a Sexless Marriage?

Sexless marriages are a common issue among couples, and it can be difficult to understand what constitutes a sexless marriage. In this article, we will explore what a sexless marriage is, the potential causes, and how to address the issue.

Defining a Sexless Marriage

A sexless marriage is defined as a marriage in which sex has not occurred for one year or more. A low-sex marriage is one in which sex occurs 10 times a year or less. Therefore, a marriage in which sex occurs roughly once a month does not meet either of these definitions.

Causes of a Sexless Marriage

There are many potential causes of a sexless marriage. Some of the most common causes include physical and mental health issues, a lack of emotional connection, or the couple's differing sex drives. Other potential causes include stress, busy schedules, financial issues, or unresolved conflicts.

Addressing a Sexless Marriage

If you are in a sexless marriage, it is important to address the issue. The first step is to talk to your partner and identify the root cause of the issue. It is also important to be honest and open with your partner and discuss any issues that may be contributing to the lack of intimacy. Additionally, it is important to make time for each other and prioritize your relationship.


Sexless marriages can be difficult to navigate, but it is important to address the issue and work towards finding a solution. It is important to talk to your partner and identify the root cause of the issue, be honest and open with each other, and make time for each other. With the right approach, it is possible to improve your relationship and increase intimacy.
