What are red flags in dating?

Dating can be an exciting and thrilling experience, but it can also be filled with potential pitfalls. Knowing what to look out for can help you identify potential red flags in a relationship before it gets too serious. Red flags in a relationship can include excessive jealousy, frequent lying, criticism, and an unwillingness to compromise.
