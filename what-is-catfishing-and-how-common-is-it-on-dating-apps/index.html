What Is Catfishing?

Catfishing is the act of creating a false identity on the internet, usually with the intention of tricking someone into a romantic relationship. The term comes from the 2010 documentary Catfish, which followed a young man's relationship with a woman he met online who turned out to be someone else entirely.

Catfishing can take many forms, from creating a fake profile on a dating app to pretending to be someone else on social media. It can also involve using stolen photos or creating a false identity to get money from someone.

• Do a reverse image search of the person's profile picture to make sure it is not stolen from another person.
